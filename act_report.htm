Dog Rating Ratio over Time¶

*Eliminated all observations for ratings that did not make sense:
    *rating_denominator != 10
    *rating_numerator > 20

Created a ratio called 'rating_ratio' and plotted the trend over the period that the twitter data was archived from 12/2015 through 8/2017.

Ratings have tended to increase over time "grade inflation" , in fact by the last month the rating ratio was over 1.2 which translates to a ratig of 12/10.

I guess this is a fun site so it is hard to make any real sense out of this other than people love thier dogs, and advertisers should taget these folks since the might have a high propensity to spend a lot on thier dogs.
